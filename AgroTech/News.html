According to which algorithm will land plots be sold at electronic auctions

On May 18, the Verkhovna Rada of Ukraine adopted the Law “On Amendments to Certain Legislative Acts Concerning the Sale of State and Communal Land Plots or Rights to Them (Leases, Superficies, Emphyteusis) through Electronic Auctions”. Unlike the current legislation, which provides for land auctions through old auctions that do not use any means of modern technology, the new law will transfer them to electronic format, where auctions are conducted online, without the physical presence of participants , and where the subjective impact on land auctions will be minimized and replaced by automatic actions of the electronic system, writes agronews.ua.

In short, the main essence of the law

Land auctions will be conducted in a single electronic trading system Prozorro. Sales in the form of an electronic auction in real time on the Internet. Based on their results, they will conclude a contract of sale, lease, superficies, emphyteusis of the land plot with the winner who offered the highest price for the land plot for sale or the highest fee for its use, fixed during the land auction.
